Volunteering

Volunteering can provide people with a new direction in life, opening up a range of activities and leading to increased self-esteem and self-confidence. It can offer the opportunity to give something back to the community or make a difference to others or volunteering can provide a gateway to develop new skills or build on existing experience and knowledge.

We have over 40 wonderful volunteers at present supporting and assisting our community in a variety of ways and we are grateful to all, past and present, who have given so much time, energy and commitment. No matter how large or small your contribution is valuable and appreciated.

There are many ways to volunteer in the local community so if you are interested then please contact us for more information.
