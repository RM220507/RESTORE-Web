What We Do

We operate the community cafe at Seaton Hospital where we work closely with a number of groups offering a warm, comfortable space in which they can run classes and workshops. See the Community Cafe page for more information.

We work closely with local businesses offering reading groups, craft sessions, workshops.

Hold meetings an collaborate with local voluntary, health and social care organisations as part of the Voluntary Community and Social Enterprise (VCSE) network in East Devon.

Attend weekly NHS Community Health & Social Care team meetings.

Actively work with local and national voluntary organisations to identify gaps in provisions with the aim to fill them.

Raise funds and support for local projects and initiatives working closely with the amazing support of Devon County Council, East Devon District Council, Seaton Town Council, Tesco Seaton, the Royal Devon NHS Trust and the local GPs. For more information on what we have been involved with go to our Projects page.

We support around 25 vulnerable households with social visits and help with shopping through our Befriending Service.

We don't pretend to know it all, that's why we work with over 40 other local groups, businesses and charities to get you the help required. If you have a community scheme or initiative that you want to develop then please contact us as we might be able to help.

Community Cafe

Come and see us in our community space at Seaton Hospital where, in partnership with Waffle, you'll receive a warm welcome and can get a great coffee.

On 7th June 2022, after permission was granted by NHS property services to re-purpose the former kitchen at the hospital, the cafe and community hub opened based on a similar concept to the successful Axminster Waffle House with the aim of combatting loneliness and encouraging community cohesion.

Every Monday and Tuesday Re:store oversee the community cafe using the space as a meeting place for a variety of local groups and organisations, with a private meeting room also available.

If you are interested in booking or utilising the space please email us or take a look at the Facebook pages for Re:Store and Waffle Seaton to find out more about local groups meeting there.
